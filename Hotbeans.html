Our Story

Who Are
Hot Beans?

Founded in 2012, Hot Beans Web is a leading web development agency based in the UK. We partner with clients across a wide range of industries — from startups to established enterprises — to design, build, and optimise digital products that make a real impact.

We're a team of developers, designers, and strategists who genuinely love what we do. Our culture is collaborative, supportive, and always curious. We invest in our people — especially those who are just starting out.

We believe the best web experiences come from diverse teams with fresh perspectives. That's why we actively recruit trainee and junior developers who bring enthusiasm, new ideas, and a hunger to grow.

Front-End

Junior Front-End Developer

Full-Time · Hybrid · Cheltenham, UK

Join our front-end team to build responsive, accessible, and visually engaging user interfaces for our growing client portfolio. You'll work closely with senior developers and designers in an agile environment, contributing to real production code from day one.

Required Qualifications & Skills

  • Level 3 qualification in IT, Computing, or related subject (or equivalent)
  • Solid understanding of HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript
  • Experience with responsive / mobile-first design principles
  • Familiarity with version control (Git / GitHub)
  • Basic knowledge of a JavaScript framework (React, Vue) is a plus
  • Portfolio demonstrating personal or coursework projects
  • Good communication skills and a collaborative mindset
